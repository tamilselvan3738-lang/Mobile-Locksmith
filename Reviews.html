Authenticity & Verification FAQ

Every review published on our site containing a 'Verified Customer' badge has been cross-referenced with a unique dispatch ticket number from our digital service scheduler. This ensures that the review is written by a real client who received emergency locksmith or installation services from a licensed member of our team.

We believe in absolute transparency. We publish all verified reviews, regardless of rating, provided they meet our community standards guidelines (no abusive language or private technician identifiers). In the rare case of negative feedback, our dispatch manager immediately opens a service ticket to resolve the customer's issues under our satisfaction guarantee.

If your situation has been resolved or you would like to update your comments, please contact our administrative desk at support@locksmith-services.com with your original ticket number, and our team will issue an edit link to update your feedback.
