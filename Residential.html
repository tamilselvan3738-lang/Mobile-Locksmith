Lock Repair & Maintenance

Precision Tumbler Diagnostics & Calibration

Sticky keys, misaligned latches, and loose handles compromise security. We disassemble lock cylinders, clean brass pins, calibrate spring tension, and lubricate components with premium graphite to restore operation.

Pin Alignment Adjustments

Micro-precision resets to match worn key shapes.

Same-Day Calibration

Fast callouts to prevent sudden home lockouts.

Key Duplication

Precision Laser Key Cutting & Re-key Copies

Inaccurate duplicates ruin lock cores over time. Our robotic laser-key machines scan original key grooves to replicate cuts with tolerances within 0.05mm, avoiding key snags.
