Cost Optimization

Rekeying Costs

Moving into a new home or lost a copy of your key? You don't need to replace the entire expensive lock body. Rekeying swaps out the internal brass tumbler pins to fit a brand new key, immediately disabling all older keys at a fraction of the cost.
