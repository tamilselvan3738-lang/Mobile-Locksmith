Standard Defense

Deadbolt Locks

The backbone of residential door security. Deadbolts resist physical force and lock picking because their bolt cannot be shimmied or retracted unless the lock cylinder is rotated using the correct physical key.

1. Keyway Cylinder Plug

The core of the deadbolt. The plug rotates when the correct key is inserted, retracting the bolt mechanism. Antigravity deadbolts feature spool or mushroom pins to resist lock-picking.

Smart Innovation

Keyless Smart Locks

Smart locks offer premium convenience with temporary passcode generation, biometric authorization, and smart home automation. Monitor access logs, issue digital guest keys, and lock/unlock your door remotely.

Featured Technology: Built with AES-256 digital encryption and anti-peep keypad screens, preventing password snooping by nearby intruders.

Automotive & commercial

Keyless Entry Systems

Keyless entry systems bypass the physical key completely. Using RF (Radio Frequency) fobs or Bluetooth-enabled smartphones, the lock detects authorization from several feet away and automatically releases the latch for hands-free convenience.

Rolling Code Technology

Every button press sends an encrypted rolling key code. The lock changes the required entry key immediately after each scan, rendering intercepted wireless cloning attacks completely useless.

Ultimate Protection

High Security Locks

Designed to withstand professional picking, drilling, and bumping attacks. High security cylinders incorporate hardened steel rods to break drill bits, complex keyways, and sidebars requiring secondary alignment pins.

Shear Line Physics

Standard cylinders unlock when all split pins align at the boundary of the rotating plug (shear line). High-security locks multiply this protection by adding sidebars and complex angle cuts on the key.

Electronic Control

Electronic & Magnetic Locks

For commercial environments requiring automated high-traffic access control. Electromagnetic locks (Maglocks) use strong electric currents to create pull forces up to 1200 lbs, keeping doors shut until power is routed away by a scanner or panic bar sensor.

Integrates with: Intercom monitors, automated scheduling, alarm bypass controllers, and facility fire exit overrides (fail-safe operation).
