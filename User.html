Rekey Cylinders Configuration

Alter internal lock pins so past keys no longer bypass security.

Rekey Specifications

Why Rekey Instead of Replace?

Rekeying is the cost-effective way to reset security. The locksmith swaps the brass tumbler pins inside the cylinder core to match a new key pattern, rendering all previous keys useless.

You don't buy expensive new lock handles or deadbolt casings—only the internal cylinders are reset.
