Code of Conduct

About FAQ Help

Operations & Security FAQs

Answers regarding our technicians, licensing, and corporate data safeguards.

Are all of your locksmiths background-checked and licensed?

Yes, absolutely. Under our corporate charter, every technician must maintain an active state locksmithing license, pass federal background checks, and undergo randomized drug screens. We carry a $2,000,000 general liability insurance policy.

How do you protect client master key code databases?

We host all commercial master key configurations, key bitting logs, and system metrics in isolated offline databases. This guarantees that your property credentials can never be leaked or accessed via standard web hacking attempts.

Do you charge different rates for emergency night calls?

No. We maintain standard emergency rates regardless of whether you call us at noon or 3 AM. Our dispatcher center will provide an exact breakdown of technician call-out rates and labor costs before sending a vehicle.
