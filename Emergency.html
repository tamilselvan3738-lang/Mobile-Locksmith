Safety Guidelines

Emergency Preparedness Checklist

What to do while waiting for your technician. Stay safe and ensure a seamless entry process.

Move to a Safe, Well-lit Area

If you are locked out late at night or in an unfamiliar neighborhood, wait in a nearby open business or under streetlights.

Prepare Proof of Occupancy

To satisfy security regulations, have your ID, mail, lease, or vehicle registration ready to verify ownership.

Clear the Path / Lock Area

Ensure the immediate doorway or vehicle side panel is accessible so the technician can deploy entry equipment immediately.

Verify Technician ID

Upon arrival, check the technician's official company uniform and locksmith licensing papers for total peace of mind.
